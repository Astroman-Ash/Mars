Basic Information About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet in the solar system . It is named after the roman god of war, Mars. Scientists theorize that at one time Mars could have had life, like Earth. Mars has two moons, Phobos and Deimos . Mars is a place that makes many people curious and fills them with wonder.

Rovers Sent to Mars

w

The rovers that have been sent to Mars are:
Sojourner (1997)
Spirit (2004–2010)
Opportunity (2004–2018)
Curiosity (2012–present)
Perseverance (2021–present)
Zhurong (2021-2022)
